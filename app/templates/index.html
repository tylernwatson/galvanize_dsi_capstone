Interactive Graphs of Twitter Mentions

Tyler Watson - Galvanize Austin Data Science Immersive

Based on approximately 420,000 tweets scraped from 11/28 - 12/14. All tweets contain '#netneutrality'.

Graphs represent networks of Twitter users mentioned together in tweets.

Edges are weighted according to the frequency their two nodes appeared together throughout the period scraped and are restricted to those with counts >= 10.

Nodes display Twitter handle on hover and link to user page. Communities are represented with color and were identified using Louvain Modularity.
