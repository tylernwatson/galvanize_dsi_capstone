Force-Directed Graphs of Twitter Mentions

Tweets scraped from 11/28 - 12/14. All tweets contain '#netneutrality'. Graphs represent networks of Twitter users mentioned together in tweets.

Edges are weighted according to frequency of two nodes appearing together throughout the period scraped.

Nodes display Twitter handle on hover and link to user page. Communities are represented with color and were identified using Louvain Modularity.
